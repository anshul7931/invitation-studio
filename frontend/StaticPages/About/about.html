About Invitation Studio

Invitation Studio is a modern platform designed to simplify the creation and sharing of digital invitations and interactive event websites.

Our goal is to provide individuals, families, businesses and event organizers with an easy-to-use platform where professional-looking invitations can be created without requiring any design or technical knowledge.

Whether you're celebrating a wedding, birthday, engagement, anniversary, baby shower, housewarming, office party, corporate event or any other special occasion, Invitation Studio helps you create memorable digital experiences that are easy to customize and share.

Our Mission

We believe every celebration deserves a beautiful beginning.

Invitation Studio aims to replace traditional static invitation cards with interactive, responsive and engaging digital experiences that can be viewed from any modern web browser.

Instead of simply sending an invitation, our platform helps users tell their story, share event information, showcase memories and connect with their guests through a seamless digital experience.

What We Offer

Invitation Studio currently supports the creation and management of digital invitations with features such as:

  • User account management
  • Secure authentication
  • Event-based invitation templates
  • Theme customization
  • Public invitation sharing
  • Invitation management dashboard
  • Interactive invitation pages
  • Responsive layouts for desktop and mobile devices

As the platform evolves, additional features may include:
