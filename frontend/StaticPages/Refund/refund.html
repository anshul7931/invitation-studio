Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

This Refund and Cancellation Policy explains how refunds, cancellations and payment-related matters are handled for Invitation Studio.

By purchasing any paid feature, subscription or service offered by Invitation Studio, you agree to this policy.

2. Nature of Our Services

Invitation Studio provides digital products and online services, including but not limited to:

  • Digital Invitations
  • Interactive Event Websites
  • Premium Templates
  • Premium Themes
  • AI-powered Features
  • Subscription Plans
  • Event Management Features
  • Other Digital Services

As these are digital products, they cannot generally be returned once delivered or activated.

3. Free Features

Many features of Invitation Studio may be available without payment.

No refund policy applies to free services.

4. Premium Features

Certain features may require payment.

Examples include:

  • Premium Templates
  • Premium Themes
  • Extended Public Link Validity
  • Advanced Customization
  • AI Features
  • Guest Management
  • Analytics
  • Additional Storage
  • Future Premium Services

These features may change over time.

5. Refund Eligibility

Refund requests may be considered only in exceptional circumstances, including:

  • Duplicate payments for the same purchase.
  • Payment processed successfully but premium features were not activated due to a verified technical issue.
  • Incorrect billing caused by an error on our side.

Each refund request will be reviewed individually.

6. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will generally not be provided in the following situations:

  • Change of mind after purchase.
  • Accidental purchase by the user.
  • Failure to use purchased features.
  • Dissatisfaction due to personal preference.
  • Expiry of invitation links or plans after the purchased validity period.
  • User-generated errors while creating invitations.
  • Incorrect event details entered by the user.
  • Deletion of invitations by the user.
  • Account suspension due to violation of our Terms and Conditions.

7. Subscription Cancellation

If Invitation Studio introduces subscription plans in the future:

  • Users may cancel their subscription at any time.
  • Cancellation will prevent future recurring charges.
  • Benefits already activated during the current billing period may remain available until the subscription expires.
  • Cancellation does not automatically qualify for a refund.

8. Payment Processing

Payments are securely processed by authorized payment service providers.

Invitation Studio does not store:

  • Credit Card Numbers
  • Debit Card Numbers
  • CVV
  • UPI PIN
  • Internet Banking Credentials

Payment information is handled directly by the payment gateway.

9. Failed Transactions

If payment fails but money is debited from your account:

  • Please wait for the payment provider or your bank to complete reconciliation.
  • In most cases, failed transactions are automatically reversed by the payment provider or your bank within the applicable processing period.
  • If the issue persists, please contact our support team with your payment details.

10. Duplicate Payments

If you believe you have been charged more than once for the same purchase, please contact us.

Verified duplicate transactions will be reviewed and, where applicable, refunded through the original payment method.

11. Processing Time

Approved refunds, where applicable, will be initiated within a reasonable period.

The time required for the refunded amount to appear in your account depends on your payment provider, bank or card issuer.

12. Future Premium Services

As Invitation Studio evolves, additional paid services may be introduced.

Specific refund terms for those services may be published separately where required.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding payments, cancellations or refunds, please contact us through the Contact Us page or the official support email provided on the platform.

Please include:

  • Registered Email Address
  • Transaction ID (if available)
  • Payment Date
  • Description of the Issue

Providing complete information will help us process your request more efficiently.

14. Changes to this Policy

Invitation Studio may update this Refund and Cancellation Policy from time to time.

The latest version will always be available on this page.

Continued use of paid services after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Thank you for choosing Invitation Studio.

We appreciate your trust and will always strive to provide a fair, transparent and secure experience for all users.
