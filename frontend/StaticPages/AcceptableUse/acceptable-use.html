Acceptable Use Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy explains the rules that apply while using Invitation Studio. Our goal is to provide a safe, secure and respectful platform for everyone.

2. Lawful Use

You may use Invitation Studio only for lawful purposes. You agree not to use the platform in any way that violates applicable laws or regulations.

3. Prohibited Content

Users must not upload, publish or distribute content that:

  • Violates any applicable law.
  • Infringes copyright or trademark rights.
  • Contains pirated material.
  • Contains malware or malicious software.
  • Promotes phishing or scams.
  • Contains obscene or sexually explicit material.
  • Promotes hatred or violence.
  • Harasses, threatens or abuses others.
  • Defames individuals or organizations.
  • Contains false or misleading information intended to cause harm.

4. Intellectual Property

Users should upload only content that they own or have permission to use. This includes:

  • Images
  • Videos
  • Music
  • SVG files
  • Logos
  • Graphics
  • Fonts
  • Event Content

Invitation Studio may remove content that appears to violate intellectual property rights.

5. Platform Abuse

You must not:

  • Attempt unauthorized access.
  • Reverse engineer the platform.
  • Bypass security controls.
  • Create fake accounts.
  • Abuse public invitation links.
  • Automate excessive requests.
  • Attempt denial-of-service attacks.
  • Exploit software vulnerabilities.

6. Spam

Users must not use Invitation Studio for:

  • Spam invitations
  • Mass unsolicited messaging
  • Fraudulent campaigns
  • Fake event creation
  • Misleading promotions

7. Public Invitations

Users remain responsible for invitations they publish publicly. Invitation Studio may disable invitations that violate this policy or applicable laws.

8. User Content

Users retain ownership of their uploaded content. However, users remain fully responsible for ensuring that their content complies with this policy.

9. Enforcement

If this policy is violated, Invitation Studio may take actions including:

  • Removing content.
  • Suspending public links.
  • Restricting certain features.
  • Temporarily suspending accounts.
  • Permanently terminating accounts.
  • Reporting unlawful activity where required by applicable law.

The action taken will depend on the nature and severity of the violation.

10. Reporting Violations

If you believe content published using Invitation Studio violates this policy, please contact us with sufficient details so we can investigate the matter.

11. Policy Updates

This policy may be revised from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.

Thank you for helping us keep Invitation Studio safe, secure and welcoming for everyone.
